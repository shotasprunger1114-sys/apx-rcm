FAQ

Questions We Get All the Time

How long does onboarding take?
Most practices are fully operational with APX RCM within 30 days. Credentialing timelines vary by payer, but billing and claim submission typically begins within the first week after EHR and portal access is granted.
Do you require a long-term contract?
No. All our agreements are month-to-month with 30 days written notice required to terminate. We've built our business around results ‚Äî not contracts.
How do you handle denied claims?
Every denied claim is tracked, categorized by denial reason, and actioned by our team. We file appeals, correct and resubmit, and dispute payer decisions when warranted. You receive a monthly denial report with trend analysis.
Is my patient data secure?
Yes. We are fully HIPAA compliant and execute a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with every client on day one. All patient and financial data remains your property at all times ‚Äî including after termination.
What EHR/PM systems do you work with?
We work with most major EHR and practice management systems. During your free revenue analysis, we'll confirm compatibility with your current setup. We use RXNT as our primary billing platform and can integrate with your existing system where supported.
What specialties do you work with?
We work with a wide range of specialty practices including primary care, urgent care, orthopedics, and more. During your free analysis call, we'll assess your specific specialty's billing requirements and payer mix.
How is APX RCM different from other billing companies?
Three things: (1) Dedicated billing manager ‚Äî you're never talking to a different person every call. (2) Month-to-month agreements ‚Äî no lock-in, ever. (3) Full transparency ‚Äî you get weekly and monthly reports so you always know where your money is.
What happens to my data if I leave?
You own all your patient and financial data ‚Äî always. Upon final payment, we provide a complete data export in your preferred format. There are no data hostage situations with us.
